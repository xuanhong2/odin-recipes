You really chose home-made butter?

Description

Well, it was an option and I'm not going to stop you. It'll take roughly 10 minutes to crate 16 servings. (That's a lot of butter)

Ingredients>

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • Â¼ teaspoon salt (Optional)

Directions

Step 1

Pour heavy cream into a food processor or blender. Process on high until butter separates, about 10 minutes

Step 2

Strain off liquid, then press butter into a small bowl with the back of a spoon to further remove liquid. Season with salt.
